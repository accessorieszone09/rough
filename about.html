    • About SDC

    Department of Computer Science Engineering & Information Technology, SAGE University Indore, Started an SAGE Developers & Professional Chapter in association with SAGE Student Ambessador, ACM, IIC & CSI.

    SDC is SAGE Developer Club,University-based community group for students interested in New developing technologies. Students from all undergraduate or graduate programs with an interest in growing as a developer are welcome. By joining a SDC, students grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their community.

    The SAGE Developers Club is here for you to introduce with all the emerging, existing technology & make you ready to start your carrier with the best of your interest in Technology.

    SDC have a lot to offer. Whether you join as a member or choose to lead one, simply taking part in an extracurricular group will enhance your experience. Gaining skills, making that's ready to take on their future career!connections, and broadening your knowledge through a Student Community.SDC will help you become a more well-rounded individual.
