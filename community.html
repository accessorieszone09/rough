    SDC is SAGE Developer Club, University-based community group for students interested in New developing technologies. Students from all undergraduate or graduate programs with an interest in growing as a developer are welcome. By joining a SDC, students grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their community.
